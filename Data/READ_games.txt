Data should be like:

a B C D E F G H I J K

where:

- a: 0 if blue team wins, 1 if red team wins
- B: top blue team
- C: jungle blue team
- D: mid blue team
- E: adc blue team
- F: supp blue team
- G: top red team
- H: jungle red team
- I: mid red team
- J: adc red team
- K: supp red team

Examples:
1 31 62 63 202 223 54 11 13 29 267
1 240 76 103 81 63 41 9 45 18 44
0 6 121 142 67 40 75 32 50 222 14
